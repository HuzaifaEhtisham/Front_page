Introduction

Prospect Motors, LLC is owned and Operated by FOCUS, LLC, after being in the car business and working for other dealers, our leadership team established Prospect Motors, LLC in 2021 to enable entrepreneurs to get into the Automotive car buying business without the costly expenses in overhead, regulators and various issues of owning a large automotive dealership.

Our firm started based on several experiences in working under other similar programs, and in the end we decided to do two things: Firstly, our team does not accept ‘anyone’ who wants to just sale cars. We are here to uh build relationships and go through great efforts to ensure we bring on the ‘right’ person as this is an industry that is built on relationships.

Second, each member who started with us has stayed with us and in turn has referred other friends, family and colleagues to our program. We are a team that is built on integrity, service before self and excellence in all that we do.

Each member will be permitted to transact either in the state in which they reside, or primarily due business in. This enables you to do what we call ‘back yard business.’ Backyard business is where you are able to go to local car auctions, conduct legal transactions and not bear the expense of transport and fees to move vehicles in and out where you are buying and selling the vehicle. Many of our members have taken advantage of leveraging social media, and other internet mediums as selling tools. During our onboarding, we will cover tips and techniques of how to maximize your profit, reduce time and remain safe when engaging with the public. As a member you will be invited to our member only portal and Community of Interest Section of our website, which will have training, blogs, templates and how to demos for you to leverage in your business.
